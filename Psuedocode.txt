COOKING FRIED EGG PSEUDOCODE

    GRAB an egg from the fridge
    SET pan onto stove
    TURN on stove to medium heat
    ADD oil onto the pan
    WAIT till oil is hot
    CRACK egg onto a small bowl
    ADD salt to taste
    POUR egg onto pan
    COOK for 2-3 minutes or until edges are crispy
    OPTIONALLY flip egg for a firmer yolk
    TURN off heat
    REMOVE egg from the pan
    PUT egg on plate
    SERVE and enjoy
